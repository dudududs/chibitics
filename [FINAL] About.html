About Us

Artist

Chibitics was founded in 2015 by Seika, a professional graphic artist based in Japan. A self-proclaimed anime and chibi enthusiast, Seika's love for these art styles inspired her to create Chibitics. The company began with adorable chibi magnet bookmarks, which is reflected in its name. Since then, Chibitics may have expanded its product line to include other chibi-related merchandise or art creations.
